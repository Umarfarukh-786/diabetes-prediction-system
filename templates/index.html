What is AI-Powered Health Intelligence AI?

AI-Powered Health Intelligence AI uses a Random Forest machine learning model trained on the PIMA Indian Diabetes Dataset — a benchmark collection of clinical measurements from thousands of patients.

Enter eight key health parameters and receive an instant, probability-weighted risk assessment. Not a diagnosis — a powerful signal for informed conversations with your doctor.
